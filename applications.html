Where Garnet Works

Applications & Industries

💧

Waterjet Cutting

80-mesh garnet is the global standard for abrasive waterjet machines cutting steel, titanium, marble and composites. Sharp angular grains give cleaner edges and higher feed rates than alluvial garnet.

🛠️

Abrasive Sandblasting

Replaces silica sand for surface preparation of ships, bridges, oil & gas pipelines and storage tanks. OSHA-friendly: free silica below 0.1%.

�

Sandpaper & Coated Abrasives

A traditional use — garnet grains bonded to paper or cloth backing for woodworking, leather and automotive sanding. Garnet self-sharpens as it fractures, giving a fine finish.

🚰

Water Filtration

Used as a high-density support layer in multimedia gravity & pressure filters in municipal, desalination and industrial process water plants.

🏗️

Surface Preparation

Concrete restoration, anti-skid flooring, rust removal, and pre-paint cleaning for bridges, tanks and structural steel.

🔬

Lapping & Polishing

Fine 120/200-mesh grades for glass, optical and semiconductor lapping where consistency is critical.

🛣️

Anti-skid Coatings

Used in deck coatings, runways, road markings and industrial flooring as a non-slip aggregate for safety surfaces.

🏭

Foundry & Casting Molds

Used as a high-density molding sand in steel and non-ferrous foundries for superior surface finish and dimensional accuracy on cast parts.

🛢️

Oil & Gas Proppants

High specific gravity (~4.0) makes garnet suitable as a hydraulic-fracturing proppant and for filtration in upstream oil & gas operations.

🏠

Roofing Granules

Coloured roofing shingles use garnet as a UV-resistant, weather-stable mineral granule with natural reddish tones.

🧪

Ceramics & Glass

Used as a high-density filler and aggregate in specialty ceramics, glass batches and refractory products.

⚡

Electronics & Semiconductors

Synthetic garnets (YIG/GGG) are critical in microwave devices, lasers and magneto-optic components — natural garnet supports lapping and polishing of wafers.

💎

Gemstone & Jewellery

Higher-grade garnet crystals — Almandine, Pyrope, Spessartine, Rhodolite — are cut as semi-precious gemstones for jewellery worldwide.

🌿

Landscaping & Aquarium Media

Decorative red-brown garnet sand is used in landscaping, terrariums and aquariums as an inert, non-toxic substrate.

🪙

Shot Peening

Used in controlled shot-peening processes to induce compressive stress on metal components, improving fatigue life of aerospace & automotive parts.

🧱

Cement & High-Density Concrete

Garnet's high specific gravity makes it valuable as a heavy aggregate in radiation-shielding concrete and counter-weight mixes.
