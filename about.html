About Us

An IRE-Backed Bridge to the World.

GarnetWorld is an authorized vendor partnered with the IRE Chhatrapur (OSCOM) plant of Indian Rare Earths Limited (IRE) — a Government of India enterprise under the Department of Atomic Energy — to bring premium beach-sand Garnet to industries worldwide. Our priority export region is East Asia (China, Japan, South Korea, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Macau, Mongolia and North Korea, subject to applicable trade regulations).
